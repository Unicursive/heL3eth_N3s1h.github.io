    HealthNest was born out of the unrelenting efforts of our Founder, to ensure affordable access to healthy, chemical free and sustainable food options to everyone.

    We began our journey in 2018 in Kerala, with the dream of offering high quality, chemical-free, unadulterated, spices and condiments, especially turmeric, grown sustainably. With the overwhelming love and support from the small group of our loyal customers, we have expanded our product range vastly.

    HealthNest offers one of the most affordable options for safe, healthy food products, with no compromise on quality. Our products are loved and sought after by customers across India, for their enviable quality.

    We invite you to join us in this exciting new journey, as we move forward, with determination, towards fulfilling the dream of a healthier India and a healthier world.
